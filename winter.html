MASHA

When you take your happiness in little bits, in snatches, and then lose it, as I have done, you gradually get coarser, more bitter.

[Points to her bosom]

I'm boiling in here....

[Looks at ANDREY with the perambulator]

There's our brother Andrey.... All our hopes in him have gone. There was once a great bell, a thousand persons were hoisting it, much money and labour had been spent on it, when it suddenly fell and was broken. Suddenly, for no particular reason.... Andrey is like that....

ANDREY

When are they going to stop making such a noise in the house? It's awful.
